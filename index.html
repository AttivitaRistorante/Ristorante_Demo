Our Story

Welcome to Golden Slice Pizzeria, where authentic Italian tradition meets California's fresh, local spirit. Since opening our doors in San Diego, we've been dedicated to crafting the perfect pizza using time-honored techniques and the finest ingredients.

Our wood-fired oven, imported directly from Naples, reaches temperatures that create that perfect crispy-yet-tender crust that pizza lovers dream about. We source our produce from local farmers, ensuring every bite is as fresh as the California sunshine.

At Golden Slice, we're not just making pizza – we're building community, one slice at a time.
